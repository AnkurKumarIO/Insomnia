# 🌐 Master Frontend Flow: AlumniConnect AI **Global UI/UX Design System** * **Aesthetic:** Modern Tech-Savvy SaaS (similar to Linear, Notion, or Vercel). * **Theme:** Dark Mode primary, minimalist layouts, soft shadows, optional glassmorphism. * **Color Palette:** Deep Blue/Indigo (Primary), Green (Success), Yellow (Warning), Red (Rejection/Error). * **Typography:** Clean sans-serif (Inter or Roboto). * **Corner Styling:** Rounded edges (12–16px radius). --- ## Phase 1: The Gateway (Landing & Authentication) ### 1. Unified Landing Page * **Layout:** Clean, centered, split-screen or three-card entry design. * **Headline/Copy:** *"Are you a Student, Alumni, or TNP Coordinator?"* * **Action:** Three distinct portal buttons routing to specific verification flows. ### 2. Role-Based Verification & Registration **A. Student Registration Flow** * **Form:** Full Name, College Email, Institution Name, College ID Number. * **Document Upload:** Drag-and-drop zone for PDF/Image of College ID/Allotment Document. * **Loading State:** *"Scanning document for authenticity..."* (Backend triggers OCR & Integrity Check). * **Status Tracker Screen:** * Account Created ✔️ * Sent to T&P ✔️ * Verification in Progress ⏳ * *Message:* "Your details are under verification by the Training & Placement Office. Credentials will be emailed upon approval." **B. Alumni Registration Flow** * **Two-Path Entry Form:** Choose between "Membership Number" or "Official Alumni Email". * **Document Upload:** Upload Degree Certificate or official ID Proof. * **Status Tracker:** Similar to the student flow, pending AI Forensic Logic to verify document authenticity. **C. TNP Coordinator Flow** * **Direct Access:** No registration. A secure login form requiring TNP ID, College Name, and College-assigned Password. Routes directly to the Admin Dashboard. ### 3. Login Page * **Layout:** Minimalist centered card. * **Fields:** Email / ID, Password. * **Extras:** "Forgot Password" link, "Remember Me" checkbox. * **Action:** Primary "Login" button. --- ## Phase 2: Student Onboarding (First Login) Upon first successful login with generated credentials, students are locked into a multi-step profile wizard. A progress bar tracks completion at the top. * **Step 1: Personal Info:** Profile photo upload (circular), Name, Bio text area. * **Step 2: Academic Info:** Institution (auto-filled), Department dropdown, Year selector. * **Step 3: Skills:** Interactive tag/chip input system (like GitHub topics). * **Step 4: Resume Upload:** Drag & drop PDF uploader with a document preview. * **Step 5: Projects:** Repeating card inputs (Project Title, Description, Tech Stack tags, GitHub link). * **Step 6: External Links:** Input fields for GitHub, LinkedIn, Portfolio, etc. * **Step 7: Career Preferences:** Role selection cards (SDE, Data Analyst, etc.) and Target Company multi-select chips. * **CTA:** *"Complete Profile"* (Unlocks the Main Dashboard). --- ## Phase 3: The Student Experience (Dashboards) ### 1. Main Dashboard (SaaS Style) * **Layout:** Left sidebar navigation (Dashboard, Profile, Alumni Network, Analytics, Resume Builder, Settings) + Main content grid. * **Top Cards:** * *Profile Strength:* Circular progress indicator. * *Career Progress:* Skill proficiency bars. * *Application Tracker:* Pipeline status (Applied → Interview → Offer). ### 2. AI Resume Analyzer Feature * **Headline:** *"Unlock Your Resume’s True Potential"* * **Sub-headline:** *"Let our AI scan your experience and perfectly align it with industry standards so you stand out to top recruiters."* * **Feature Cards (Bullet Points):** * *Instant Scoring:* Get a clear strength rating out of 100. * *Target Company Match:* Discover which companies are looking for your exact skill set. * *Actionable Fixes:* Step-by-step suggestions on formatting and keyword optimization. * *ATS Optimization:* Ensure your resume passes automated recruiter filters. ### 3. Progress Analytics Dashboard * **Headline:** *"Your Growth Journey"* * **Sub-headline:** *"Track your mock interview performance, identify weak spots, and watch your confidence soar."* * **Graph Section:** A continuous line graph plotting 'Interview Score' over 'Interview Number'. * *Tooltip:* "Hover over a point on the graph to see a quick snapshot of that specific interview." * **Actionable Insights Checklist:** A chronological list of past interviews below the graph. Clicking an interview expands an interactive checklist of AI/Mentor feedback. * *Instructions:* "Check off these AI-generated action items as you practice them in real life to improve your next score!" * **Empty State:** *"No interviews yet! Book your first session with an alumnus to jumpstart your analytics graph."* ### 4. Alumni Discovery & Booking (Impact Dashboard) * **Top Filter Bar (Sticky):** Dropdowns and chips for Company, Domain, Experience Slider, Skills, and Branch. * **Alumni Grid:** Responsive tiles (Avatar, Name, Role/Company, short summary, and a visual "Impact Score" out of 100). * **Expanded Profile View:** Full-page layout showing Bio, Socials, 'Resource Vault' (downloadable links), and an 'Impact Dashboard' (bar chart of 'Students Benefited'). * **Booking Modal (Slot Selection):** Calendar view. If slots are empty/booked, displays a *"Request Custom Slot"* button to ping the Admin/Alumni. --- ## Phase 4: The Alumni Experience (Mentorship Hub) ### 1. Alumni Dashboard * **My Schedule (Calendar):** * *Description:* "Manage your availability. Greyed-out slots are already booked." * **Interview Requests:** * *Description:* "Students waiting for your guidance." * *Empty State:* "You're all caught up! No pending requests right now." * *Card UI:* Displays Student Name, ID, Links, Full Resume, and an **AI-Generated Summary Resume**. * **Request Actions:** * *CTA Buttons:* "Accept & Schedule" or "Decline". * **Scheduled Interview Actions:** * *CTA Buttons:* "Reschedule Session" (Secondary), "Start Interview" (Primary - only active at exact scheduled time). * **Post-Interview Evaluation (Appears immediately after call ends):** * *UI:* Star rating for Overall Score, text area for 'Positive Feedback', and text area for 'Areas for Improvement'. * *CTA Button:* "Submit Evaluation". --- ## Phase 5: The Dual-Agent Interview Room (The Core Feature) This is the live video environment where the hackathon "magic" happens. * **Central Layout:** A clean 1-on-1 video call grid. Minimalist control bar at the bottom (Mute, Video Off, Share Screen, End Call). * **Student View (The Coach):** * *Overlay:* A floating, semi-transparent widget. * *Metrics:** Live 'Confidence Meter' (progress bar based on eye contact/movement) and a 'Clarity Gauge' (based on rate of speech/filler words). * **Alumni View (The Whisperer):** * *Sidebar:* A private, read-only chat feed titled *"AI Whisperer"*. * *Content:* Live-updating cards showing 'Suggested Follow-up Questions' and 'Technical Fact-Checks' based on the student's real-time audio transcript. --- ## Phase 6: The TNP Coordinator Experience (Admin Control) * **TNP Dashboard:** A master control center. * **Verification Queue:** A list of pending Student and Alumni registrations flagged by the AI for final human approval. * **Placement Readiness Heatmap:** Aggregate data showing overall student performance scores. * **System Oversight:** Ability to view platform statistics, assign custom slots, and manage database integrations.
